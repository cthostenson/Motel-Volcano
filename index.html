About Us

Motel Volcano is located in the Northwestern part of Fortnite Island, and offers a beautiful view of Mount DeFault, the active Volcano on the island. We’ve proudly served our customers since 2017 and offer a multitude of fun on the island. From leisurely drives in our comfortable golf carts to hiking up the titanic Mount DeFault, there’s no shortage of fun to be had at Motel Volcano!
